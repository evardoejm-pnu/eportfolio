Reflection

Serving as a teaching assistant provided a unique vantage point to witness the synergy between instructional design and classroom management. My time with Dr. Castro emphasized that effective teaching is a practice of constant refinement—adjusting the pace of a demonstration or the clarity of a prompt based on immediate student feedback. This experience transitioned me from a spectator to an active contributor in the learning process.
