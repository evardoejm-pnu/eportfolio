AY 2025-2026 Term 2 | Webinar Series Reflection

A collection of insights and reflections gathered from the CIVEr webinar series, exploring the intersection of professional excellence, spiritual mending, and the systemic realities of the teaching profession.

Webinar Artifacts

Webinar 1: CIVEraging on Truth (Harmony of the Soul)
View Full Reflection

This reflection centers on the "Casting and Mending" framework. It explores how the relentless demands of the teaching profession can break a "net" that is not intentionally restored. Moving from the high-pressure environment of DepEd to research, I reflect on how personal restoration is not a luxury, but a spiritual and physical necessity for a sustainable career.

Webinar 2: CIVEraging on Truth (Performance-Driven Educators)
View Full Reflection

Focused on the DepEd Performance Management System (PMES), this piece contrasts the ideal of Classroom Observations as "tools for growth" with the harsh reality of "ticking boxes". I reflect on the struggle to maintain pedagogical identity within a rigid system and advocate for a shift from a "trap-like" observation culture to one rooted in genuine, safe conversation.
