Each entry represents a milestone in the journey toward becoming a proficient educator within the Philippine Professional Standards for Teachers (PPST) framework.

PPST Proficient Teacher Competency Survey
Reflection:

Completing this assessment was a humbling experience that highlighted the transition from being a "subject expert" in tertiary education to becoming an effective teacher in the DepEd system.
