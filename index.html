My PBCTP Journey

Greetings! My name is Ezekiel John M. Evardo. I am currently enrolled in the Professional Bachelor of Certification in Teaching Program (PBCTP) while proudly serving as a Provisional Senior High School Teacher I for the DepEd Schools Division Office (SDO) of Parañaque. This e-Portfolio is a curated digital space documenting my parallel journey of practical teaching and academic growth in the Philippine Normal University (PNU) Manila.

This platform maps my transformation into a qualified professional, showcasing artifacts—from detailed lesson plans and assessment tools to philosophical reflections and visual documentation—that demonstrate my mastery of core teaching standards. This portfolio is designed to be a comprehensive and transparent record of my commitment to effective, student-centered learning and professional development. I invite you to explore the evidence of my dedication to the field of education.
